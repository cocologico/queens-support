Delete Your Account

Queens: Puzzle — Coco Lógico

You can delete your Queens: Puzzle account and all associated data at any time:

From the app

Go to Settings → "Delete my account" and confirm. This immediately and permanently deletes your account, username, and game progress.

Without the app installed

Email us at cocologico.juegos@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account" and the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Account email, display name, username, and all game progress (puzzle completion times, daily streaks, and campaign progress). No data is retained after deletion.
